// WHO WE ARE

ABOUT US

TRADE & TRACK is a centralized visual hub designed to reconnect people with their lost belongings through community collaboration. Finders can instantly document discovered items with photos and descriptions, while owners create detailed alerts about when and where items went missing — turning a stressful loss into a fast, successful reunion.

// OUR MISSION

Connecting People Through Recovery

Our mission is to maximize the chances of reunion by fostering a transparent, high-integrity network for lost and found property. By organizing reports into a searchable interface, the platform eliminates the guesswork of traditional recovery. We believe technology can reduce stress, improve communication, and bring people back to their belongings.

// TARGET AUDIENCE

Who Is This For?

This platform is built for the general public — anyone who has lost a valuable item and anyone who has found one. Whether you're a student, commuter, or community member, TRADE & TRACK gives you the tools to report, search, and recover.
